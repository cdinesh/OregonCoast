Oregon Coast & Crater Lake Adventure Itinerary: July 4–9

Overview: Scenic road trip through Oregon’s coast, Crater Lake NP, and Central Oregon. Maximize daylight, avoid night driving, and capture stunning sunsets.

Day 1: July 4 – Northern Oregon Coast to Tillamook

Astoria Column, Cannon Beach, Neahkahnie Viewpoint, Tillamook Creamery, Cape Meares sunset. Lunch at Mo’s Seafood or Wayfarer Restaurant. Lodging at The Tillamook Inn. Drive: ~95 mi, 2.5 hrs.

Day 2: July 5 – Central Oregon Coast: Tillamook to Florence

Three Capes Loop, Cape Kiwanda, Yaquina Head, Devil’s Punchbowl, Cape Perpetua, Thor’s Well. Sunset at Cape Perpetua. Dining: Mo’s or Luna Sea Fish House. Lodging at Driftwood Shores. Drive: ~100 mi, 3.5 hrs.

Day 3: July 6 – Florence to Chemult (Crater Lake NP)

Heceta Head Lighthouse, Hobbit Trail, Oregon Dunes, Sea Lion Caves. Afternoon drive to Crater Lake for Rim Drive views. Lodging at Eagle Crater Lake Inn. Drive: ~140 mi, ~3 hrs.

Day 4: July 7 – Crater Lake NP to Bend

Morning at Crater Lake, optional Cleetwood Cove hike, visit Newberry Volcanic Monument (Lava Butte, Paulina Peak). Bend brewery tour & dinner. Lodging: Holiday Inn Express. Drive: ~135 mi, 3 hrs.

Day 5: July 8 – Bend to Portland

Tumalo Mountain hike, Deschutes River walk, drive via Columbia River Gorge. Multnomah Falls hike, sunset at Crown Point. Lodging at Kimpton RiverPlace. Drive: ~180 mi, 4.25 hrs.

Day 6: July 9 – Portland & Departure

Morning in Portland, optional Mt. Hood trip (if time). Return car, depart by 6 PM.
