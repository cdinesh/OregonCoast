Oregon Coast & Inland Adventure: 5-Day Itinerary

Overview: Designed to maximize daylight and scenic routes. Key locations include beaches, viewpoints, lighthouses, waterfalls, and iconic coastal highways.
Sunset ETA Goal: Arrive at each lodging before ~8:45 PM

Day 1 (July 4) – Tillamook

Lodging: The Tillamook Inn, 2515 Main Ave N | Conf #: H7H171WDL
Key Stops & Activities:
  • Cannon Beach: Haystack Rock, ocean views
  • Neahkahnie Viewpoint + Hike: 1.5 hrs South Trail
  • Tillamook Creamery: Tour, samples, ice cream
  • Three Capes Scenic Loop: Cape Meares, Lookouts
  • Cape Kiwanda: Dory boat landings, climb dunes
Drive: 95 miles, ~2 hr 30 min
Dining: Lunch – Wayfarer; Dinner – Pelican Brewing
Sunset: Cape Kiwanda

Day 2 (July 5) – Florence

Lodging: Driftwood Shores Resort | Conf #: EXP-2210820413
Key Stops & Activities:
  • Devil’s Punchbowl: Tide-dependent access
  • Yaquina Head Lighthouse: Tide pools at Cobble Beach
  • Cape Perpetua: Devil’s Churn, Thor’s Well, Spouting Horn
  • Heceta Head Lighthouse: Short hike + Hobbit Trail
Drive: 100 miles, ~3 hr 30 min
Dining: Local Ocean Seafoods; Waterfront Depot
Sunset: Heceta Head Viewpoint

Day 3 (July 6) – Chemult

Lodging: Eagle Crater Lake Inn | Conf #: 2210823968
Key Stops & Activities:
  • Coquille Point: Scenic stop in Bandon
  • Port Orford: Fish & Chips stop
  • Samuel H. Boardman Corridor:
    • Natural Bridges Viewpoint
    • Secret Beach (1.5 hr hike)
Drive: 160 miles, ~4 hr 30 min
Dining: Bandon Fish Market; Crazy Norwegian’s
Sunset: Secret Beach

Day 4 (July 7) – Bend

Lodging: Holiday Inn Express, Bend | Conf #: 62279082
Key Stops & Activities:
  • Crater Lake NP: Rim Drive, Cleetwood Cove Trail (1.5 hrs)
  • Fort Rock: Optional geological stop
Drive: 135 miles, ~3 hr 15 min
Dining: Brunch – Crater Lake Lodge; Dinner – Deschutes Brewery
Sunset: Crater Lake Rim Viewpoint

Day 5 (July 8) – Portland

Lodging: Kimpton RiverPlace Hotel | Conf #: 29291103
Key Stops & Activities:
  • Mt Hood: Trillium Lake, Timberline Chairlift
  • Multnomah Falls: Arrive ~4:30 PM, hike upper trail (1.5 hrs)
  • Scenic Drive: Historic Columbia River Hwy
Drive: 180 miles, ~4 hr 15 min
Dining: Lunch – Timberline Lodge; Dinner – Departure (Portland)
Sunset: Crown Point Vista
